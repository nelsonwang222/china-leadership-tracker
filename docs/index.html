What this is

Xinwen Lianbo (新闻联播) is China's flagship 7 p.m. news program, simulcast nationally by CCTV. Its running order and wording are a curated official record of what the party-state wants known about its leaders: who met whom, who inspected what, which meetings were chaired, and which policy language was used. This site turns the full text of every broadcast since February 2016 into a structured, searchable, English-accessible database, updated automatically every day.

How it is built

  • Collection. Each day's transcript is downloaded from CCTV's public program pages shortly after broadcast.
  • Extraction. Every news item is scanned against a roster of ~58 senior figures (Politburo members of the 18th–20th Central Committees, state leaders, and key ministers). Items are classified by rule into event types — meetings, bilateral talks, inspection tours, chaired meetings, phone calls, congratulatory messages, foreign trips, ceremonies, NPC/CPPCC deliberations, and more.
  • Translation. English titles and one-to-two-sentence analytic summaries are produced with a large language model and cached; the original Chinese text is always preserved and shown alongside.
  • Network. The co-appearance graph counts how often two roster figures appear in the same news item — a standard proxy for elite proximity used in scholarship on Chinese politics.

Caveats for researchers

  • The source is state media: coverage reflects official signaling, not the full universe of leadership activity. Absence from Xinwen Lianbo is itself a signal, but interpret it carefully.
  • Event typing is rule-based on headline patterns; a small share of items are classified as other or coverage (thematic propaganda pieces that mention a leader without reporting an activity). The "leader activities only" filter excludes those.
  • Name matching is by exact Chinese name; a leader mentioned by title only (e.g. 总书记 without 习近平) is not captured in that item.
  • English summaries are machine-generated and should be checked against the Chinese original before quotation.

Data access

The underlying data is plain JSON, republished on every update: data/index.json (all events), data/leaders.json (roster), and data/events-YYYY.json (full Chinese text by year). Cite as "China Leadership Tracker, based on CCTV Xinwen Lianbo transcripts."
